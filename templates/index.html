Sasha Milyakina

Sashami a.k.a. Sasha Milyakina is a research fellow in Semiotics at UT and a freelance artist who captures Tartu's architectural heritage in art form. Starting from 2017, she has created calendars inspired by the city. Her clients include the University of Tartu, Tartu City Government, Tartu City Museum, Setomaa museums and many more. Sasha is from Moscow and has been living in Estonia since 2013.
